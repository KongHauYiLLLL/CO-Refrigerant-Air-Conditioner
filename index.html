CO₂ Refrigerant Air Conditioner

GWP1: The New Standard

Why CO₂ Refrigerant?

🌍

Protect Nature

CO₂ (R744) has a Global Warming Potential (GWP) of 1, compared to up to 2,000 for common refrigerants.

⚡

Optimized for Tropical Conditions

Optimized for tropical climates like Malaysia, maintaining efficiency even under extreme heat.

🛡️

Safe Refrigerant

CO₂ is non-toxic and non-flammable, making it a safer alternative to traditional refrigerants.

Engineering Challenges

⚙️

High Pressure

Operates at up to 5× the pressure of conventional systems, requiring reinforced components.

📐

Complex Design

Requires careful engineering and precise thermal management for optimal performance.

💰

Higher Cost

Slightly higher upfront cost, but long-term environmental savings are substantial.

Why Now?

🌡️

Global Cooling Demand Rising

Cooling demand is rapidly increasing due to urbanization and rising temperatures worldwide.

🔥

Heat Waves Increasing

Extreme heat events are becoming more frequent, increasing the need for sustainable cooling solutions.
