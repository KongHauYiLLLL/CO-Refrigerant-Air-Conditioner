CO₂ Refrigerant Air Conditioner

GWP1: The New Standard

Why CO₂ Refrigerant?

🌍

Protect Nature

CO₂ (R744) has a Global Warming Potential (GWP) of 1, compared to up to 2,000 for common refrigerants.

⚡

Optimized for Heat Recovery and Colder Climates

Optimized for Colder Climates, and excellent for Heat Recovery in Industrialization & Commercialization.

🛡️

Safe Refrigerant

CO₂ is non-toxic and non-flammable, making it a safer alternative to traditional refrigerants.

Engineering Challenges

⚙️

High Pressure

Operates at up to 5× the pressure of conventional systems, requiring reinforced components.

📐

Complex Design

Requires careful engineering and precise thermal management for optimal performance.

💰

Higher Cost

Slightly higher upfront cost, but long-term environmental savings are substantial.

Technical note: CO₂ systems operate transcritically near the critical point at 31.1°C. Above this threshold, heat rejection/gas-cooling conditions can make efficiency harder to maintain unless the system is designed with heat recovery in mind.
