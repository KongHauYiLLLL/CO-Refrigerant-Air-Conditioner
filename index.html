Why is the CO₂ Refrigerant Air Conditioner worth your time?

Protect Nature

CO₂ (R744) has a Global Warming Potential (GWP) of 1, compared to up to 2,000 for common refrigerants.

Efficient in Hot Climates

Optimized for tropical climates like Malaysia, maintaining efficiency even under extreme heat.

Safe Refrigerant

CO2 is non-toxic and non-flammable, making it a safer alternative to traditional refrigerants.

How it Works

A CO2 air conditioner uses carbon dioxide as the refrigerant instead of traditional synthetic gases. The CO₂ circulates in a closed high-pressure loop where it is compressed, cooled in a gas cooler, expanded through a valve, and then absorbs indoor heat before repeating the cycle.

But what challenges are there when making the CO₂ Refrigerant Air Conditioner?

Costs More

CO₂ systems currently cost more to design and manufacture, but their long-term environmental benefits are substantial.

Harder to Design

CO₂ systems can reach up to 5x higher pressure than traditional air conditioners.

Why Now?

Global Cooling Demand Rising

Cooling demand is rapidly increasing due to urbanization and rising temperatures worldwide.

Heat Waves Increasing

Extreme heat events are becoming more frequent, increasing the need for sustainable cooling solutions.

Where?

High-rise Buildings

Efficient centralized cooling for dense urban environments.

Data Centers

Reliable high-performance cooling for energy-intensive computing infrastructure.

Aircraft Ground Cooling

Sustainable cooling systems for aircraft during ground operations.
